Baked Potatoes

Baked potatoes are such an easy to bake meal, the skin is nutritious for you and crispy when not wrapped in foil during the baking process. You can add whatever you want after slicing it open, stuffing its insides with cream cheese, sour cream, pepper you name it!

Ingredients

  • However many potatoes you will want to use
  • Olive oil
  • Sea or Kosher salt
  • An Oven

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325F
  2. Scrub the potatoes well and pat them dry with a kitchen towel
  3. Poke the potatoes with a fork a few times to let it vent so it won't blow up!
  4. Place them on a baking sheet and rub them all over with olive oil
  5. Sprinkle your salt on the potatoes
  6. Bake for 45 to 60 minutes
  7. Dig in!
